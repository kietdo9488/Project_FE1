One place for all your goals​

ECharts is built on the popular Chartist.js library, which is known for its flexible settings, wide charting capabilities, and adaptability. You can add the required amount of data, adjust the grid display, and change the colors, fonts, paddings, and margins for elements. All this can be configured directly from the Elementor’s interface and does not require additional code knowledge.
Compatibility

Cross-Browser Support

ECharts is exceptionally compatible with all modern browsers. It does not matter which browser users view your site: Chrome, Firefox, Opera, Safari, or Edge. Everything will look great on all devices and in all browsers.
